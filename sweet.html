Room Description

Sweet Spring Suite is located on the second floor, and has easy access to the front porch, with a private entrance and wraparound balcony overlooking Spring Street. The suite is furnished with a queen size bed, a full bath with double Jacuzzi/shower combination, an inviting bed with soft linens and dazzling fixtures of leaded glass lamps for warmth and romance. The suite also includes a small refrigerator with complimentary water and soft drinks, Keurig coffee maker, microwave, glasses, separate sitting area, flat screen TV with cable, free Wi-Fi, Air Purifier and beautiful antique furnishings.

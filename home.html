Welcome to Elmwood House Historical Inn of Eureka Springs

Located in downtown Eureka Springs

Elmwood House is a unique historical inn that offers luxurious, yet comfortable, lodging. The historic Elmwood House was established in 1886 and is located at the north end of downtown Eureka Springs, AR. The property is on the north end of historic downtown district where shops, fine dining, clubs, art galleries, historic sites, and entertainment are located within easy walking distance.

Cancellation Policy 

Elmwood House is a small inn and cancellations impact us significantly. When we guarantee an accommodation, we make that particular unit unavailable to others for the duration of the booking. With only four units, a single cancellation can have a very big impact on our business. With that in mind, our policies are outlined below:

Cancellation Policy: If a change to reservation or cancellation is made 15 or more days prior to reservation there will be a $25 processing fee and the balance of the deposit will be refunded. Reservations cancelled less than 15 days but more than 2 days prior to arrival will forfeit their one night deposit. If cancellation is received within 2 days of the reservation the full amount of the stay will be charged.

Inn Policies
